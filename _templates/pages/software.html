Software

GRG has developed two innovative software projects, DIPY and FURY. DIPY (Diffusion Imaging in Python) is an open-source library for analyzing diffusion MRI data, helping researchers study brain connectivity. FURY is a 3D visualization tool designed for creating advanced scientific visualizations. Both projects demonstrate GRG's commitment to providing robust solutions for the scientific and medical communities.

DIPY

DIPY is the paragon 3D/4D+ imaging library in Python. Contains generic methods for spatial normalization, signal processing, machine learning, statistical analysis and visualization of medical images.

FURY

FURY is designed to simplify the creation of advanced scientific visualizations and animations, making it more accessible for researchers to present complex data in an intuitive and visually compelling way.
